Mode 2
On-Site Assessment — Maturity Scan D2
Score each question based on what you observe. Notes are optional but recommended.
Pillar 01
Advanced Manufacturing Technologies
Capability to deploy and operate production technologies that enhance process precision, flexibility, and efficiency, building on stable and standardised processes
—
1.1
Are production technologies deployed on stable, standardised processes — or do they compensate for process instability?
1.2
How consistently are production technologies used across shifts and operators?
1.3
Is equipment capability matched to current product and process requirements?
1.4
Is there evidence that technology investments have improved consistency, throughput, or resource efficiency?
1.5
Is advanced manufacturing (additive, flexible automation, collaborative robotics) in use or planned?
Pillar 02
Digital Factory
Capability to capture, access, and use operational data across the organisation, including in fragmented data environments
—
2.1
Can operational data (production, inventory, quality) be accessed and combined across functions without manual reconciliation?
2.2
Is there a single version of truth for key operational metrics?
2.3
How current and reliable is operational data at the point of decision?
2.4
Are ERP, MES, or equivalent systems in use and actively used by operations teams?
2.5
Where formal integration is absent, are context-based or bridging approaches used to combine data?
Pillar 03
Eco Factory
Capability to monitor, manage, and reduce environmental impact and resource use within production systems
—
3.1
Is energy consumption measured at the process or machine level?
3.2
Is waste and scrap quantified, categorised, and linked to specific production activities?
3.3
Are resource efficiency targets set and tracked with defined KPIs?
3.4
Is environmental data used in operational decisions — not just reported?
3.5
Is there evidence of circular resource flows — reuse, recycling, or material recovery?
